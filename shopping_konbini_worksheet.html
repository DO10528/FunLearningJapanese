コンビニ会話 ワークシート

Convenience Store Conversation Practice

名前 (Name):
1. お支払い (Payment)
店員 (Clerk)
How would you like to pay?
あなた (You)
Cash, please.
あなた (You)
By credit card.
あなた (You)
By electronic money.
2. お弁当 (Bento Box)
店員 (Clerk)
Would you like your bento warmed up?
あなた (You)
Yes, please.
あなた (You)
No, thank you.
3. お箸・スプーン (Cutlery)
店員 (Clerk)
Do you need chopsticks?
あなた (You)
Yes, one pair please.
あなた (You)
Spoon, please.
4. レジ袋 (Plastic Bag)
店員 (Clerk)
Do you need a plastic bag?
あなた (You)
Yes, please.
あなた (You)
I'm fine.
5. レシート (Receipt)
店員 (Clerk)
Do you need a receipt?
あなた (You)
Yes, please.
あなた (You)
No need.
6. 質問してみよう (Ask a Question)
あなた (You)
Excuse me, is there a toilet?
あなた (You)
Can I exchange money?
あなた (You)
Do you have meat buns?
あなた (You)
Can I have a wet towel?
